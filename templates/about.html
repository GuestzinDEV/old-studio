About Old Studio

Old Studio is a small Brazilian game development team focused on creating experiences for the Roblox platform.

Game Philosophy

All of our games are completely free to play. Players do not need to spend Robux in order to fully enjoy our experiences.

We strongly value storytelling and attention to detail. Each game we create has a background story, and some of our projects take place within the same shared universe.

Supporting the Studio

We offer optional donation game passes for players who wish to support our work. Any Robux donated helps us improve our games even further.

To make a donation, simply enter one of our games and purchase one of the available donation game passes.
